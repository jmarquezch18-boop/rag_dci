We present MultiSpanQA, a reading comprehension dataset where answers consist of multiple discrete spans.

As part of this, we proposed a method for classifying the semantic structure of answers, based on the semantic relation between answer spans.

We also provide an expanded version of the dataset which includes unanswerable questions and single-answer questions, to make it both more challenging and more realistic.

We additionally presented a number of models for multi-span QA extraction, and found that the best-performing model was sequence tagging-based, augmented by a span number prediction module and span adjustment module.
