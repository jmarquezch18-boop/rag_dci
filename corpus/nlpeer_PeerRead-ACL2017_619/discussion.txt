While it is well-established that thoughtful revisions improve one's writing, and while many college-level courses require students to submit multiple drafts of writing assignments (Addison and McGee, 2010), instructors rarely monitor and provide feedback to students while they revise.

This is partly due to instructors' time constraints and partly due to their uncertainty about how to support students' revisions (Cole, 2014;Melzer, 2014).

There is much we do not know about how to stimulate students to self-reflect and revise.
